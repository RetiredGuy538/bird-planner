Scoring guide

What's New in v2.0

  • 🌕 Solunar tables — major/minor feeding windows from lunar position math
  • 🌕 Prime Session tier — golden hour + calm + major solunar all aligned
  • 🎙 Haikubox panel — live detections from your Lake Koshkonong device
  • Scoring: light 40 · golden hour 30 · solunar +20 · all normalized to 100

Wind Logic

  • Deck faces west over Lake Koshkonong
  • Easterly winds (E, NE, ENE): shielded ~70% — effective wind much lower
  • Western winds (W, NW, SW): full exposure — raw wind applies
  • ⛨ = sheltered (easterly wind direction)

Solunar Science

  • 🌕 Major periods: moon overhead or underfoot (±1 hr) — peak feeding
  • 🌙 Minor periods: moonrise / moonset (±1 hr) — moderate feeding
  • Aligned with golden hour = 🌕 Prime Session designation
  • Calculated entirely from lunar position math — no external API

Score Breakdown

  • 🌕 Prime: golden hour + calm + major solunar — rarest, best sessions
  • 75–100 Excellent — set up blind tonight
  • 55–74 Good — worth setting up same morning
  • 35–54 Fair — casual observation only
  • Wind 40 + Light 40 + Sun 30 + Dry 10 + Solunar 20 → scaled to /100
