Scoring guide

Wind Logic

  • Deck faces west over Lake Koshkonong
  • Easterly winds (E, NE, ENE): deck shielded ~70% — effective wind much lower
  • Western winds (W, NW, SW): full exposure — raw wind applies
  • ⛨ = sheltered indicator (easterly origin)

Blind Setup Advice

  • Green banners = 2+ consecutive hours scoring ≥50
  • Plan blind setup the evening before multi-hour morning windows
  • Below 10 mph effective wind = optimal for stationary blind
  • Sheltered easterly mornings = best for keeping blind up

Eagle Watch Conditions

  • Easterly wind (calm, sheltered water surface)
  • Morning hours — bald eagles hunt with sun behind them
  • Clear to partly cloudy — bright water reveals fish
  • Look toward sunrise-lit shoreline trees

Score Breakdown

  • 75–100: Excellent — set up blind tonight
  • 55–74: Good — worth setting up same morning
  • 35–54: Fair — casual observation, skip the blind
  • 0–34: Poor — wind or light unfavorable
